Mediawiker 2.3.9:

* Mediawiker specific shortcuts:
 * **ctrl+b** - bold
 * **ctrl+i** - italic
 * **ctrl+alt+[2, 3, 4]** - heading of 2, 3 and 4 levels
 * **f5** - reopen page (refresh)
 * **ctrl+s** - post page (optional, check new option **mediawiker_wiki_instead_editor**)
* Possibility to create new shotcuts in mediawiker context (**mediawiker_is_here**) - for examples, check sublime-keymap files.
